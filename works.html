02 : Life

According to several futurist theorists, human life on Earth and in space will be feasible thanks to technological advances. These and many other seemingly crazy ideas shape the conception of our life in 2050.

Global temperature could rise up to three degrees

We will colonize Mars and the Moon, and we will be able to exploit asteroids as common land mines.

Many of the elements that are scarce on Earth can be used thanks to this new space environment.

We will go to space on vacation, and there will be a very important competition from all countries to be the leader.

Cars will no longer have a driver and will be driven by computer.

Many species of plants and animals will disappear.
