05: PROJECTS

The reincarnation of people through AI

Raymond Kurzweil, says that we will be able to "bring back" our relatives through artificial intelligence. He says that by 2050, we will be able to send nanobots into people's brains to extract memories of loved ones.

Wearing active contact lenses

Think of a Google Glass (augmented reality device that projects directly to the eye a virtual image that is superimposed on the real image in front of us) but now in contact lenses.

Downloading dreams

By scanning brain activity it is already possible to decipher what someone is looking at. By the year 2050, it may be possible to actively monitor or download dreams, which makes one wonder if one day it will also be possible to upload false memories.
