03: SPACE

The Sun will emit less light and heat to the Earth

total solar irradiation is expected to be reduced to 0.25% over a 50-year period between 2020 and 2070, peaking in 2050. The magnification effect produced by a grand minimum is only a fraction of the temperature increase caused by the concentration of carbon dioxide. Therefore, solar minimum will not prevent the Earth from continuing to warm, although it may slightly slow down the rate of warming.

Colonization of space will be possible by 2050

The United States is already testing organ delivery by drone to speed up the process. Google has obtained authorization to operate its UAV project, Safran is also positioning itself... This ecosystem is in the process of organizing itself and laying the foundations for what will be useful airspace in galactic zones in the future.

Exoplanets tempered to the Milky Way

This mission aims to study distant objects within our galaxy, both exoplanets with the possibility of harboring life and the less known objects of the Milky Way such as interstellar matter or dark matter. It is left for the future to choose between the study of the less known regions of our galaxy and the study of exoplanets located in the habitability zone of their solar systems.
